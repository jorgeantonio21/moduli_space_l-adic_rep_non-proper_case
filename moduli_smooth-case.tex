\begin{abstract}
In this text we prove that if we take $G$ a more general profinite group, for example an absolute Galois group, $G$, the moduli $\mathrm{LocSys}_{G,n, \Gamma}$ is representable by a rigid $\ell$-analytic space, provided we fix the inertia action at infinity.
\end{abstract}
